Our Company's History

The company was incorporated January 31, 1898, upon the merger of 17 pulp and paper
in the northeastern United States. Its founders and first two presidents were William Augustus Russell, who died suddenly
in January 1899, and Hugh J. Chisholm.[6] Philip Tell Dodge, president of the Mergenthaler Linotype Company, served as its chairman for 11 years.
The invention of the Linotype dramatically increased the size of newspapers and the need for newsprint. The newly formed company supplied 60 percent of all newsprint in the country.
